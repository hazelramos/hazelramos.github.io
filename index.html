Google Maps API Integrated to McDonald’s Delivery Website


A systems integration project which observes and proposes the benefits of how Google Maps API can enhance McDonald’s Delivery website as well as other food delivery websites or applications. This study or project can give ideas to companies who offer express service that deliver through the use of a website or application on how Google Maps API can enhance their delivery services which then can greatly earn their consumers’ trust. With the use of Google Maps API, a web-based map can show the locations of nearby McDonald’s and the travel distance and journey duration between the path of the pickup (McDonald’s restaurant) and dropoff (customer’s location) destinations. In today’s on-demand world, timely and speedy delivery plays a key role in customer satisfaction. With accurate mapping resources, organizations are finding ways to eliminate precious minutes from last-mile delivery routes so customers keep coming back.

Role: Front-end and Back-end Developer
Technologies used: Google Maps API, Bootstrap
